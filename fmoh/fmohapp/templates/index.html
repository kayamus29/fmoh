FMOH&SW PURPOSE

The Federal Ministry of Health & Social Welfare, Nigeria, is dedicated to safeguarding and improving the health and well-being of all Nigerians. Guided by our vision, mission, and mandate, we strive to create a robust healthcare system that ensures accessibility, affordability, and quality care for every citizen. Through strategic policies, innovative programs, and strong partnerships, we are committed to advancing public health, strengthening healthcare infrastructure, and promoting social welfare across the nation.

Our Vision

To reduce the morbidity and mortality due to communicable diseases to the barest minimum, having minimal prevalence of non-communicable diseases, meeting global targets on the elimination and eradication of diseases, and significantly increasing the life expectancy and quality of life of Nigerians.

Our Mission

To develop and implement policies and programmes as well as undertake other necessary actions that will strengthen the national health system to be able to deliver effective, efficient and affordable health services that foster improved health status of Nigerians, serving as the engine for the pursuit of accelerated economic growth and sustained development.

Our Agencies

The Federal Ministry of Health & Social Welfare works in collaboration with various agencies to implement health policies, regulate medical standards, and enhance healthcare delivery across Nigeria.
