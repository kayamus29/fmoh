About the Permanent Secretary

KACHOLLOM SHANGTI DAJU, mni, is the Permanent Secretary of the Federal Ministry of Health and Social Welfare. She began her public service career with the Federal Ministry of Information, now known as the Federal Ministry of Information and National Orientation (FMI & NO), as an Information Officer II (GL 08) and rose to the rank of Director (Information) in January 2019.

Current Role

Ms. DAJU KACHOLLOM mni was appointed as a Federal Permanent Secretary for the Federal Ministry of Labour and Employment by His Excellency, President Muhammadu Buhari, on April 27, 2022. Prior to this appointment, she served as the Director of Public Relations and Protocol at the Federal Ministry of Information and Culture.

Roles and Responsibilities

The Permanent Secretary is the Chief Accounting Officer of the ministry and is responsible for running the ministry's day-to-day activities. Her specific functions include:

  • Providing support and advice to the Honourable Ministers
  • Ensuring proper implementation of ministry-specific policies, budget plans, projects, and strategic plans
  • Providing leadership through the management and motivation of Directors and reform initiatives to increase overall work efficiency
  • Coordinating the effective and efficient utilization of financial resources in line with the Ministry of Finance/Treasury requirements and policies as well as the Procurement Act
  • Effectively managing human resources; instilling discipline, supporting innovations and inventions, and creating an enabling environment for staff growth and self-improvement
  • Managing communications and interactions with the public
  • Consulting with Directors and DGs of agencies and parastatals under the ministry where appropriate
