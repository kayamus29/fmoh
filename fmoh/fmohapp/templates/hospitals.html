Overview of Hospitals

The Federal Ministry of Health oversees a network of hospitals that provide specialized, tertiary, and comprehensive healthcare services across Nigeria. Below are summaries of the hospital categories:

Specialty Hospitals

We have 13 Specialty Hospitals dedicated to providing specialized medical care in areas such as orthopedics, cardiology, ophthalmology, and more. These hospitals cater to specific health needs and offer advanced treatments.

View Specialty Hospitals

Teaching Hospitals

Our 20 Teaching Hospitals serve as centers of excellence for medical education and research. These institutions train healthcare professionals while delivering high-quality patient care.

View Teaching Hospitals

Federal Medical Centers

There are 22 Federal Medical Centers spread across the country, offering comprehensive healthcare services to the public. These centers play a crucial role in improving access to quality healthcare nationwide.
