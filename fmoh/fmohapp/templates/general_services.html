Mandate of General Services Department

The mandate of the General Services Department in the Federal Ministry of Health encompasses various responsibilities related to the efficient management of resources and services within the ministry. These include:

  • Organizing and managing logistics and supplies within the ministry.
  • Conveying important operational information to stakeholders and staff.
  • Managing relationships with vendors and suppliers.
  • Building and maintaining a positive operational culture within the ministry.
  • Facilitating effective communication across departments.
  • Overseeing the use of cost-effective platforms and technologies for resource management.
  • Developing and implementing strategies for handling emergencies and crises.
  • Supporting the ministry's policy advocacy efforts through efficient service delivery.

Vision

To institutionalize a maintenance culture with a view to minimizing waste.

Mission

To use cost-effective means in ensuring that government assets are maintained and serviced at regular intervals.
