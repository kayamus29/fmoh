LEGAL SERVICES UNIT

The Legal Services Unit of the Federal Ministry of Health is an extension of the Chamber of the Attorney General of the Federation and Minister of Justice; hence it serves as its liaison office.

MANDATE

The mandate of the Legal Services Unit is to provide high-quality legal services to the Federal Ministry of Health and all its Parastatals and Agencies...

VISION

To offer quality legal services to the Federal Ministry of Health.

MISSION

To ensure that the Federal Ministry of Health is legally protected in its quest to deliver quality healthcare services to all Nigerians.

STRUCTURE

The Unit is headed by the Legal Adviser who is a Director and is assisted by Assistant Legal Advisers...

FUNCTIONS

  • Proffering legal advice and opinions to the Ministry, its Agencies, and Parastatals;
  • Legal representation of the Ministry in courts and tribunals;
  • Preparation and vetting of Contract Agreements;
  • Drafting and vetting legislation for the Ministry;
  • Representation at public Hearings at the National Assembly;
  • Vetting and release of Advance Payment Guarantees (APGs);
  • Serves as the Freedom of Information (FOI) Desk of the ministry;
